About Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is the annul sports fest of RCCIIT. With the motto to perform better than our predecessors. This year we are putting spontaneous efforts towards achieving new heights of fun, games, and entertainment. With an active team of organizers we have planned a plethora of entertaining, brotherly sporting events under the collective banner of Game of Thrones..
